About Me

Hi, my name is Ekene and I am an aspiring Data analyst. I’m looking for a position that will allow me to use my data analytical skills in helping to make a decision. A little bit on my background, I have a BS in ME, where many of the projects I led and were a part of , heavily depended on being able to analytically make inferences from the data. After graduation I hopped on an opportunity to work for one of the largest residential solar companies in the country and I took it. Over the past few years I have been teaching myself python--after finding the pandas library, and more recently am enrolled in the full time data analytics immersion with ThinkFul.

I’ve been strengthening these skills through my in depth studies of Excel such as power tables and pivots , ETL with SQL, and Python programming. Eventually I’d like to master my statistical and programming skills and welcome any questions you may have about my experiences or what I could bring to your organization.
